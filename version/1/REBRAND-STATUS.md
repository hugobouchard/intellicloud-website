## Core Messaging Framework (IMPLEMENTED)

### Brand Positioning
- Mission: We engineer revenue, not just build software
- Positioning: "The SWAT team for enterprise growth"
- Value Prop: Business outcomes, not features
- Differentiator: Complete tracking infrastructure proving which dollar created which deal

### Tone Guidelines
- Active voice: "We architect" not "We offer"
- Outcome-focused: "Reduce costs by 40%" not "Improve efficiency"
- Specific terms: "Executive Dashboards" not "Reports"
- Enterprise language: "Integration" not "Connection", "Architecture" not "Design"
- Avoid: Marketing fluff, vague benefits, jargon without context
