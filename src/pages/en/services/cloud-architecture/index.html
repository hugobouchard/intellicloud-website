Cloud Migration FAQ: Your Questions Answered

Everything you need to know about cloud architecture, migration costs, and working with IntelliCloud

How much does cloud migration cost?

Cloud migration projects typically start at $25,000 for small to medium applications and can range up to $500,000+ for complex enterprise systems. At IntelliCloud, we offer transparent pricing with a free architecture audit to provide accurate estimates. Most clients see ROI within 12-18 months through reduced infrastructure costs (40% average savings), eliminated hardware refresh cycles, and improved operational efficiency.

How long does a cloud migration take?

Migration timelines vary based on complexity. Simple applications can be migrated in 4-6 weeks, while enterprise systems typically take 8-16 weeks. Our phased approach ensures zero downtime during transition, with comprehensive testing at each stage. We provide detailed project timelines during the architecture assessment phase.

What are the risks of cloud migration?

Common migration risks include data loss, downtime, cost overruns, and security vulnerabilities. IntelliCloud mitigates these through our proven 6-phase methodology: comprehensive assessment, detailed architecture design, staged migration with rollback capabilities, extensive testing, security hardening, and post-migration optimization. We've completed 150+ cloud migrations with zero data loss and zero unplanned downtime.

Which cloud platform is best - AWS, Google Cloud, or Azure?

The best cloud platform depends on your specific needs. AWS offers the most comprehensive service catalog (200+ services) and largest market share, making it ideal for most enterprise workloads. Google Cloud excels in AI/ML capabilities, data analytics with BigQuery, and Kubernetes (GKE). Azure integrates seamlessly with Microsoft ecosystems. We provide unbiased platform recommendations based on your technical requirements, team expertise, and business objectives.

Will cloud migration save money or cost more?

When properly architected, cloud infrastructure typically reduces total cost of ownership by 40-60%. Savings come from eliminating hardware purchases, reducing data center costs, optimizing resource utilization through auto-scaling, and decreasing IT staff overhead. However, poorly designed cloud architectures can cost more than on-premise systems. Our architecture review includes detailed cost modeling to ensure positive ROI before migration.

Do you provide ongoing support after migration?

Yes, we offer comprehensive managed cloud services including 24/7 monitoring, performance optimization, security management, cost optimization, and dedicated support teams. Our CloudOps packages start at $2,500/month and include proactive monitoring, monthly optimization reports, and guaranteed response times. Many clients see 15-25% additional cost savings in the first year through our continuous optimization.

How do you ensure security and compliance in the cloud?

Security is built into every layer of our cloud architecture. We implement industry best practices including encryption at rest and in transit, multi-factor authentication, network segmentation, automated security scanning, and continuous compliance monitoring. We have expertise in HIPAA, PCI-DSS, SOC 2, GDPR, and other compliance frameworks. All architectures include disaster recovery and business continuity planning with defined RPO/RTO targets.
