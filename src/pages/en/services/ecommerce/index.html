eCommerce Development FAQ

Common questions about Shopify Plus development, costs, and our process

How much does Shopify Plus development cost?

Shopify Plus development typically ranges from $15,000 to $75,000 depending on customization needs. Basic Shopify stores start at $8,000-$15,000, while complex custom eCommerce platforms can cost $75,000-$200,000+. IntelliCloud provides transparent pricing with detailed project scopes. The investment typically pays for itself within 6-12 months through increased conversion rates and average order values.

Should I use Shopify, Shopify Plus, or a custom platform?

Shopify is perfect for businesses doing under $1M in annual revenue with straightforward needs. Shopify Plus is ideal for $1M-$50M+ businesses requiring advanced customization, automation, and wholesale capabilities. Custom platforms are best for complex B2B requirements, unique workflows, or businesses with very specific technical needs. We assess your business model, technical requirements, and growth plans to recommend the best solution.

How long does it take to build an eCommerce store?

Timeline varies by complexity: Shopify stores take 4-8 weeks, Shopify Plus implementations take 8-16 weeks, and custom platforms take 12-24 weeks. Our agile approach delivers working features iteratively, so you can start selling sooner. We provide detailed project timelines during the discovery phase.

What's your average conversion rate improvement?

Our eCommerce stores average 35% higher conversion rates compared to industry standards through optimized UX design, fast load times (under 2 seconds), mobile-first approach, and conversion-focused checkout flows. We also implement A/B testing and continuous optimization to improve conversions over time. Many clients see 2-3x revenue increases in the first year.

Do you provide ongoing support and maintenance?

Yes, we offer comprehensive eCommerce support packages starting at $1,500/month including security updates, performance monitoring, conversion optimization, A/B testing, and priority support. Our growth packages also include marketing automation setup, abandoned cart recovery optimization, and quarterly strategy sessions. Most clients see 15-25% revenue growth year-over-year with our ongoing optimization.

How do you handle payment processing and security?

We integrate industry-leading payment gateways (Shopify Payments, Stripe, PayPal, and more) with PCI-DSS compliant security. All stores include SSL certificates, fraud detection, secure checkout, and compliance with international payment regulations. We also implement 3D Secure authentication, address verification, and advanced fraud prevention rules to protect your revenue.

Can you migrate my existing store to Shopify or Shopify Plus?

Yes, we specialize in eCommerce migrations from WooCommerce, Magento, BigCommerce, and custom platforms to Shopify/Shopify Plus. Our migration process includes complete data transfer (products, customers, orders, SEO data), URL redirects for SEO preservation, design improvements, and zero downtime cutover. We've completed 200+ successful migrations with an average 25% conversion rate improvement post-migration.
