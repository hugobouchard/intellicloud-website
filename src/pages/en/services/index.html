Frequently Asked Questions About Our Software Development Services

How is IntelliCloud different from other software development agencies?

We're revenue engineers, not feature factories. While other agencies code to spec, we architect systems designed to drive measurable business outcomes. Every project starts with business impact discovery, not feature lists. We own the outcomes, not just the deliverables.

What's your typical project timeline and cost?

Project timelines range from 8 weeks (MVP) to 6+ months (enterprise platforms). Costs vary based on scope: $25K for small integrations, $100K-$500K for full platform builds. We provide fixed-price quotes after a discovery phase. Most projects achieve positive ROI within 12-18 months.

Do you provide ongoing support after launch?

Yes. We offer 24/7 managed support starting at $3K/month, including monitoring, performance optimization, security updates, and emergency response. Unlike agencies that disappear post-launch, we're your long-term technical partner. All projects include 90 days of post-launch optimization.

What industries do you specialize in?

Our core expertise is B2B SaaS, eCommerce, and financial services. We've delivered 500+ projects across manufacturing, healthcare, professional services, and technology sectors. If you're processing transactions, managing complex data, or scaling operations, we've solved similar challenges.

Can you take over an existing project or codebase?

Absolutely. We specialize in rescue missions: auditing legacy code, refactoring technical debt, and scaling systems built on shaky foundations. Our first step is always a comprehensive technical audit to identify quick wins and long-term architecture improvements.

How do you ensure projects stay on budget and timeline?

Weekly sprint demos, real-time project tracking, and transparent communication. You see exactly what's built every week. We use agile methodology with fixed sprints and clear deliverables. If scope changes, we document the business impact and cost implications before proceeding.

What technology stacks do you work with?

We're platform-agnostic and choose tech based on business needs: AWS/GCP for cloud, React/Next.js for web, React Native for mobile, Node.js/Python for backends, Salesforce for CRM. If your team already has a tech stack, we work with it. If you're starting fresh, we recommend proven, scalable technologies.
