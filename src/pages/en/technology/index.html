Frequently Asked Questions About Enterprise Technology

Which CRM platform is right for my business: Salesforce or HubSpot?

Salesforce is ideal for complex B2B sales processes, enterprise customization, and organizations with dedicated admins (10+ licenses). HubSpot works better for smaller teams (5-50 licenses), simpler workflows, and marketing-first organizations. We audit your needs and recommend based on actual requirements, not vendor preferences.

How long does a typical CRM or cloud implementation take?

CRM implementations: 8-16 weeks for Salesforce (depending on complexity), 4-8 weeks for HubSpot. Cloud migrations: 6-12 weeks for AWS/GCP infrastructure. AI integrations: 4-8 weeks for custom agents. We provide detailed project timelines after discovery phase.

Can you integrate our existing systems with new technology?

Yes. We specialize in complex integrations: ERP to CRM, legacy databases to cloud platforms, custom APIs connecting disparate systems. Most integrations take 2-6 weeks depending on data complexity and API availability.

What's the real cost of AI implementation (beyond the software)?

AI agent implementations range from $15K (simple chatbot) to $150K+ (custom workflow automation). Costs include: custom development, data preparation, prompt engineering, integration with existing systems, and ongoing optimization. Most clients see ROI within 6-12 months from automation savings.

How do you ensure our team actually adopts new technology?

Our implementations include: hands-on training workshops, role-specific documentation, change management planning, executive sponsorship alignment, and 90-day post-launch support. We measure adoption rates and optimize workflows based on actual usage data. Average 95% adoption after 90 days.

What ongoing support do you provide after implementation?

We offer managed services starting at $2K/month: platform administration, user support, system optimization, security updates, and monthly performance reviews. Unlike consultants who disappear post-launch, we're your long-term technology partner.

Can you audit our current technology stack and identify improvements?

Yes. Our Technology Stack Audit ($5K, refundable if we proceed with implementation) includes: system architecture review, integration analysis, cost optimization opportunities, security assessment, and ROI projections for improvements. Most audits identify 20-40% cost savings.
