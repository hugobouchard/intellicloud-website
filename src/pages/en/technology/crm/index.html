Frequently Asked Questions

Salesforce or HubSpot—which CRM is right for my business?

Salesforce fits enterprise companies ($10M+ revenue) needing deep customization, complex workflows, and unlimited scalability. HubSpot suits SMBs and mid-market companies wanting ease-of-use, fast deployment, and all-in-one marketing+sales. We'll assess your needs during discovery.

How long does a CRM implementation take?

Small business implementations: 6-8 weeks. Mid-market: 12-16 weeks. Enterprise with extensive customization: 20-24 weeks. Timeline depends on data migration complexity, number of integrations, and custom development requirements.

Do you migrate data from our existing CRM or spreadsheets?

Yes. We extract data from your existing system (Salesforce, HubSpot, Pipedrive, Excel, Google Sheets, etc.), clean duplicates and inconsistencies, map fields to your new CRM structure, and import with full data integrity validation.

What happens if we outgrow our CRM choice?

We design implementations with growth in mind. Most clients scale within their chosen platform (e.g., HubSpot Starter → Professional → Enterprise). If you need to migrate platforms later, we handle the transition—though proper initial selection minimizes this.

Can you integrate our CRM with other tools?

Absolutely. We integrate CRMs with marketing automation, email platforms, accounting software (QuickBooks, Xero), support systems (Zendesk, Intercom), e-commerce platforms (Shopify, WooCommerce), and custom APIs. We've built 500+ integrations.

Do you provide training for our team?

Yes. All implementations include role-based training (sales reps, managers, admins), video tutorials, documentation, and 30 days of post-launch support. We ensure your team can use the CRM confidently from day one.

What's the ROI of a proper CRM implementation?

Average clients see 29% sales productivity increase, 27% shorter sales cycles, and 40% reduction in manual data entry. One enterprise client reduced support ticket resolution time by 40%, cutting support costs by $850K annually. ROI typically achieved within 6-12 months.
