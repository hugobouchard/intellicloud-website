Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a HubSpot implementation take?

Starter setup: 4-6 weeks. Professional implementation: 8-12 weeks. Enterprise ecosystem: 14-18 weeks. Timeline depends on Marketing Hub complexity, email template design, workflow automation scope, and team training needs.

Is HubSpot easier to use than Salesforce?

Yes. HubSpot is designed for ease of use with a modern, intuitive interface. Most teams reach proficiency in 2-3 weeks vs 6-8 weeks for Salesforce. HubSpot is ideal for SMBs and mid-market companies; Salesforce is better for complex enterprise needs.

Can HubSpot replace both our CRM and marketing automation?

Absolutely. HubSpot CRM is free forever (unlimited users). Marketing Hub replaces tools like Mailchimp, Marketo, or Pardot. Sales Hub replaces standalone CRMs. Most companies consolidate 3-5 tools into HubSpot, saving $2K-$5K/month in software costs.

Do you migrate data from our old CRM and marketing tools?

Yes. We migrate contacts, companies, deals, email history, and campaign data from any CRM (Salesforce, Pipedrive, Zoho) or marketing platform (Mailchimp, ActiveCampaign, Constant Contact). We clean data, deduplicate records, and preserve all historical activity.

Can HubSpot integrate with our other tools?

Yes. HubSpot has 1,000+ native integrations: Salesforce, Google Workspace, Microsoft 365, Slack, Zoom, QuickBooks, Shopify, WordPress, and more. We also build custom integrations via HubSpot API for proprietary systems.

Do you provide ongoing HubSpot support?

Yes. After implementation, we offer HubSpot managed services retainers ($2K-$6K/month) for ongoing email campaign management, workflow optimization, dashboard updates, new integrations, and monthly strategy calls.

What's included in HubSpot training?

Role-based training for marketers (email campaigns, landing pages, workflows), sales reps (pipeline management, sequences), and admins (reporting, integrations). Includes live sessions, recorded videos, documentation, and 30-day post-launch support.
