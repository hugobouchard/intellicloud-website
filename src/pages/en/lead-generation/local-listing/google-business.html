Frequently Asked Questions

What is Google Business Profile and why does it matter for local search?

Google Business Profile (formerly Google My Business) is your business listing that appears in Google Search and Google Maps when people search for local services. It matters because 46% of all Google searches have local intent, and 76% of people who search for something nearby visit a business within 24 hours. A properly optimized profile drives phone calls, direction requests, and website visits—completely free organic traffic from high-intent local customers.

How is Google Business Profile optimization different from regular SEO?

Traditional SEO focuses on ranking your website pages in organic search results. Google Business Profile optimization focuses on appearing in the local 3-pack (map pack)—the three local business listings that appear at the top of search results with a map. This prime real estate appears above organic results and captures clicks from users with immediate local intent. Different ranking factors include proximity, categories, reviews, and profile completeness instead of traditional SEO signals.
