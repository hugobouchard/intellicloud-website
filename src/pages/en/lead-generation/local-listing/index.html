Why Local Businesses Stay Invisible

Inconsistent Business Information

Your address is different on Google vs. Yelp vs. Facebook. Inconsistent NAP (Name, Address, Phone) confuses search engines and tanks your rankings.

Zero Review Strategy

You have 8 reviews while competitors have 200+. Google favors businesses with high review volume and fresh reviews. You're losing to competitors solely because of review count.

No Multi-Location System

You manage 10, 50, or 500 locations manually. Listings are outdated, hours are wrong, and some locations don't even exist in Google. It's chaos.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to see results from local SEO?

Most clients see improved local pack visibility within 60-90 days. Full results—including top 3 map pack rankings and increased foot traffic—typically manifest in 4-6 months. Local SEO is cumulative: the longer you optimize, the more dominant you become.

What's the difference between local SEO and regular SEO?

Local SEO focuses on geographic-specific searches ("plumber near me," "restaurants in Seattle") and map pack rankings. It requires optimizing Google Business Profile, managing citations across 80+ directories, generating location-specific reviews, and building local backlinks. Regular SEO targets broader organic rankings.

How do you handle multiple locations?

We create unique Google Business Profiles for each location, ensure NAP (Name, Address, Phone) consistency across all directories, generate location-specific reviews, build individual landing pages per location, and optimize each listing for local keywords. Our platform manages 850+ locations simultaneously.

Do you guarantee first-page rankings?

No one can ethically guarantee #1 rankings—Google's algorithm changes constantly. However, we deliver measurable results: 94% of clients achieve top 10 local pack rankings within 6 months for their primary keywords. We focus on driving actual business results—calls, visits, and revenue—not just rankings.

What's NAP consistency and why does it matter?

NAP = Name, Address, Phone number. Your business information must be identical across Google, Yelp, Facebook, Apple Maps, and 80+ other directories. Inconsistent listings confuse Google and hurt rankings. We audit, clean, and synchronize your NAP across all platforms.

How do you handle negative reviews?

We implement a review response strategy: acknowledge concerns professionally, offer to resolve issues offline, and demonstrate commitment to customer satisfaction. While we can't remove legitimate negative reviews, we can bury them with a volume of positive reviews (4-5 star goal). We also monitor for fake reviews and submit removal requests when appropriate.

Can you help with franchises or multi-location chains?

Absolutely. We specialize in multi-location management. Our largest client has 850 locations optimized simultaneously. We handle bulk listing management, franchise-compliant branding, location-specific review generation, and centralized reporting across all properties.
