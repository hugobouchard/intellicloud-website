Frequently Asked Questions

What is technical SEO and why does it matter?

Technical SEO optimizes your website's infrastructure for search engine crawling and indexing. It matters because even great content won't rank if Google can't efficiently crawl, understand, and index your pages. Technical barriers like slow load times, crawl errors, or mobile issues can tank your rankings regardless of content quality. Our clients average 98 Lighthouse scores and zero crawl errors.

What's the difference between technical SEO and on-page SEO?

Technical SEO focuses on site infrastructure—speed, crawlability, mobile optimization, indexing, and Core Web Vitals. On-page SEO focuses on content optimization—titles, headers, keyword usage, and content quality. Both are essential. Think of technical SEO as the foundation and plumbing of your house, while on-page SEO is the interior design. We optimize both.

What's included in your technical SEO service?

Our technical SEO service includes: (1) Site speed optimization for sub-3-second load times, (2) Mobile optimization and responsive design validation, (3) Crawlability and indexing optimization including robots.txt and XML sitemaps, (4) Structured data implementation for rich snippets, (5) Core Web Vitals optimization for LCP, FID, and CLS, and (6) Technical audit with ongoing monitoring and issue resolution.

How long does technical SEO take to show results?

Technical improvements often show faster results than content strategies. Site speed improvements can boost rankings within 1-2 weeks as Google recrawls your pages. Crawl error fixes typically show results within 2-4 weeks. Core Web Vitals improvements can impact rankings immediately after the next Google update. Most clients see measurable ranking improvements within 30-60 days of technical optimization.

Can technical SEO help if I'm already ranking well?

Absolutely. Technical SEO can be the difference between page 1 position 5 and position 1. Even sites ranking well often have technical issues holding them back—slow mobile performance, inefficient crawl budget usage, or missing structured data. We've helped clients jump from position 3-5 to position 1 purely through technical optimization, resulting in 2-3x more organic traffic from the same keywords.
