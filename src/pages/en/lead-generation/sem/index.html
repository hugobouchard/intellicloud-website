Our Revenue-Focused PPC Framework

1

High-Intent Keyword Strategy

We target bottom-funnel keywords with clear buying intent: "PPC management services," "Google Ads consultant," "hire PPC agency." These searches convert at 8-12%, versus 1-3% for top-funnel terms.

Example: Instead of bidding on "digital marketing" ($45 CPC, 1.2% CVR), we target "B2B SaaS PPC management" ($67 CPC, 11% CVR)—lower volume, 10x better ROI.

2

Salesforce Integration for Closed-Loop Attribution

Every click is tracked from Google Ads → landing page → lead → opportunity → closed deal. We use Salesforce Campaign Influence to attribute revenue to specific keywords and ads.

You see exactly which campaigns generated $500K in pipeline vs. which wasted budget on junk leads.

3

Continuous Optimization & A/B Testing

We test 5-10 ad variations per week, adjust bids daily based on performance data, and ruthlessly cut underperforming keywords. Most agencies test once per quarter—we test constantly.

One client saw CPA drop from $420 to $180 in 6 weeks through aggressive creative testing.

4

Landing Page Optimization

We design conversion-optimized landing pages aligned to each campaign. Clear value proposition, single CTA, trust signals, and mobile-first design. Most agencies send traffic to your homepage—we build dedicated conversion engines.

Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in your PPC management fee?

Our monthly management fee covers campaign strategy, keyword research, ad copywriting, bid optimization, A/B testing, conversion tracking setup, Salesforce integration, monthly performance reports, and dedicated account management. You control your ad spend separately.

How much should I budget for Google Ads spend?

We recommend a minimum ad spend of $5,000/month to generate statistically significant data. Most clients spend $15K-$50K monthly on ads (separate from our management fee). We'll provide a recommended budget based on your target CPA and revenue goals.

How do you integrate with Salesforce?

We track every lead from initial click through closed deal using Salesforce Campaign Influence. This closed-loop attribution shows exactly which keywords, ads, and campaigns drive revenue—not just leads. You see true ROI, not vanity metrics.

What's your typical ROAS?

Our average client achieves 3.5x return on ad spend within 90 days. One SaaS client generated $220K in pipeline from $65K ad spend (3.4x ROAS). Results vary by industry, average deal size, and sales cycle length.

Do you manage platforms beyond Google Ads?

Yes. We manage Google Ads, Microsoft Ads (Bing), LinkedIn Ads, and YouTube advertising. For B2B clients, we often recommend a multi-platform approach targeting decision-makers across channels.

What's your contract length?

Minimum 3-month commitment, then month-to-month. PPC requires 60-90 days to optimize and generate reliable data. Most clients stay 18+ months as they scale campaigns profitably.

How do I know if PPC is right for my business?

PPC works best when: (1) your customer lifetime value exceeds $1,500, (2) you have a clear sales process, (3) you can track leads to revenue, and (4) you're willing to invest 3+ months in optimization. We'll assess fit during your consultation.
