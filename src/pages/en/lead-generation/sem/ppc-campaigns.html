CASE STUDY: B2B Service Company

A B2B consulting firm was spending $25,000/month across Google Ads ($12k), LinkedIn Ads ($8k), and Bing Ads ($5k). Each platform managed separately by different team members. No unified tracking. LinkedIn generating awareness but couldn't prove conversions. Google getting last-click credit for everything. Bing Ads ignored despite low CPCs.

We unified their campaigns: implemented cross-platform conversion tracking with proper attribution, discovered LinkedIn drove 60% of first touches but Google got all conversion credit, reallocated budget based on true multi-touch attribution, created coordinated remarketing across all platforms, and optimized creative per platform based on what actually worked.

Results: Results in 90 days: Overall ROAS increased from 2.1x to 4.8x, total leads increased 127% (same budget), properly attributed LinkedIn's contribution—shifted budget from $8k to $14k/month, reduced Google spend from $12k to $8k (still same leads), and scaled Bing from $5k to $3k (better ROI on Google). Total investment: same $25k/month. Revenue increase: $1.4M annually.

Frequently Asked Questions

Which PPC platforms should I use?

Depends on your audience and goals. B2B services: LinkedIn + Google. E-commerce: Google Shopping + Facebook/Instagram. Local services: Google + Bing. We analyze your target audience, competition, and customer journey to recommend optimal platform mix. Most clients run 2-4 platforms for best reach and redundancy.

How do you handle cross-platform attribution?

We implement multi-touch attribution tracking across all platforms. Track full customer journey from first touch to conversion. Use attribution models (first-touch, last-touch, linear, time-decay) to understand each platform's true contribution. This prevents over-crediting last-click platforms and under-investing in awareness channels.

Can you manage campaigns we've already built?

Absolutely. We audit existing campaigns, identify quick wins, and optimize while maintaining what works. Most clients see 30-50% ROAS improvement in first 90 days just from better optimization and budget allocation of existing campaigns.

How is PPC different from just Google Ads?

PPC (Pay-Per-Click) encompasses all paid search and social advertising—Google, Bing, LinkedIn, Facebook, etc. Google Ads is just one platform. Multi-platform PPC delivers better results through audience diversity, platform redundancy, and optimized budget allocation based on where your customers actually are.

What's your minimum budget?

We recommend $10,000/month minimum across all platforms to gather meaningful data and optimize effectively. Below that, you don't have enough budget to test multiple platforms properly. If budget is under $10k, focus on 1-2 platforms maximum. Our management fee is 12-18% of total spend depending on budget size.

How do you measure success?

Five key metrics: (1) Overall ROAS across all platforms, (2) Platform-specific ROAS to optimize budget allocation, (3) Cross-platform CPA showing true acquisition cost, (4) Platform-specific conversion rates to identify optimization opportunities, and (5) Customer journey analysis to understand platform roles. You receive unified dashboard with all metrics.

What's your pricing?

We charge 12-18% of total monthly ad spend depending on budget size and complexity. Minimum management fee: $2,000/month. Setup fee: $3,500 for new clients (includes cross-platform tracking setup, campaign build, and attribution configuration). No long-term contracts.
