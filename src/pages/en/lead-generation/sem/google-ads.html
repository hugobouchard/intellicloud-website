CASE STUDY: B2B SaaS Company

A B2B SaaS company was spending $15,000/month on Google Ads but couldn't prove ROI. Their previous agency delivered 2,400 clicks/month but only 12 demo requests (0.5% conversion rate). CPA was $1,250—far above their $300 target. They had no idea which keywords drove actual customers.

We rebuilt their campaigns from scratch: implemented proper conversion tracking linked to CRM, eliminated broad match keywords wasting 40% of budget, restructured campaigns around product categories, rewrote ad copy to focus on specific pain points and outcomes, created dedicated landing pages for each ad group, and implemented Smart Bidding with target CPA of $300.

Results in 90 days: Conversion rate increased from 0.5% to 7.2%, CPA decreased from $1,250 to $287, demo requests increased from 12 to 86 per month (617% increase), and same $15,000 budget now generating 5.8x more qualified leads. Customer acquisition cost decreased 77%. Annual revenue impact: $840,000.

Frequently Asked Questions

What makes your Google Ads management different?

We optimize for revenue, not clicks. Most agencies maximize spend to boost their fees. How much should I spend on Google Ads?

Budget depends on your industry, competition, and goals. We recommend starting with $3,000-5,000/month minimum for B2B services to gather meaningful data and optimize. E-commerce typically starts at $5,000-10,000/month. Our management fee is 15-20% of ad spend. We focus on scaling what works rather than burning through arbitrary budgets.

What's included in your Google Ads service?

Complete campaign management: (1) Keyword research and competitive analysis, (2) Campaign structure and ad group organization, (3) Ad copywriting and A/B testing, (4) Bid strategy and budget optimization, (5) Landing page recommendations and conversion optimization, (6) Conversion tracking setup with revenue attribution, and (7) Weekly performance reports with optimization recommendations. Everything needed to generate qualified leads profitably.

How long until I see results from Google Ads?

You'll see traffic immediately, but meaningful optimization takes 30-60 days. Week 1-2: Campaign launch and initial data collection. Week 3-4: First optimization round based on early performance data. Month 2-3: Significant performance improvements as we optimize based on conversion data. Most clients achieve target CPA and ROAS by month 3. Unlike SEO, Google Ads delivers immediate visibility—but optimal performance requires data-driven optimization.

Do you work with all industries?

We specialize in B2B services, professional services, SaaS, and e-commerce. We don't work with industries where we can't deliver measurable ROI (local services with low budgets, highly regulated industries with advertising restrictions). How do you measure success?

Four key metrics: (1) ROAS (Return on Ad Spend)—revenue generated per dollar spent, (2) CPA (Cost Per Acquisition)—how much each customer costs, (3) Conversion Rate—percentage of clicks that convert, and (4) Quality Score—Google's measure of ad relevance and landing page quality. You receive weekly reports with all metrics, plus month-over-month trends. Average client results: 4.2x ROAS, $2.18 CPA, 8.7% conversion rate.
